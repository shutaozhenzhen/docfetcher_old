How to receive notification of new releases

DocFetcher is hosted on SourceForge.net, the largest repository of Open Source software on the web. Creating a SourceForge.net account is free and allows you to "monitor" your favorite SourceForge.net projects, that is, to receive a notification e-mail when a new version of the respective program is released.

To receive notification e-mails for new releases of this program, first register on SourceForge.net, then go to this site and click on the tiny "monitor" button on it.
