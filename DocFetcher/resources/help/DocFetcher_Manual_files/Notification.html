How to receive notification of new releases

To receive a notification e-mail on new releases of this program, first register on SourceForge.net, then monitor the DocFetcher package. This can be done by clicking on the small "Monitor this package" button on this site. This of course works with all the other projects on SourceForge.net as well.

Registration on SourceForge.net is free and will not increase the amount of incoming spam in your e-mail account (unless you opt in to receive newsletters or something...).
